This portal is a prototype of tourist management system developed by two sophomores from Vellore Institute of Technology (Norbu Gyeltshen and Chembian RK). Although this was simply meant to be a project for Database Management system as part of our curriculum, we took it a step further to develop into something more practical that can be implemented in the real world using Web development tools such as HTML, JSS and CSS, and database tools such as PHP and MySQL.
